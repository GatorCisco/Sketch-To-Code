Dive in Mexico

The pinnacle of scuba diving in Mexico is probably the remote, hard to get to and protected Banco Chinchorro. This coral atoll is off the coast of Costa Maya not far from the border between Belize and Mexico. Join us and amaze yourself diving with turtles in the most amazing scenarios of the Caribbean Reef.
